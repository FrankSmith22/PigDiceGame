Welcome to PIG! A game about rolling dice
Two players take turns rolling a dice, where the face of the dice determines your accumulating points(your turn total). However, rolling a 1 at any point sets your turn total back down to 0, so it is sometimes wise to hold your points permanently(your overall total) so they will not be lost, and then the turn will pass to the other player. First to 100 points wins!
